Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Marco Farina APP ("the App") handles your information when you use our Canva application.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data entered in the App (name, surname, phone number, email address, company, role, website) is processed exclusively within your browser and within the Canva editor environment.

2. How We Use Your Information

The contact information you enter is used solely to generate a vCard QR code and business card layout within your Canva design. This data is never saved, shared, or transmitted outside of your current session.

3. Data Storage

The App does not use databases, cookies, or any form of persistent storage. All data is temporary and exists only during your active session in the Canva editor.

4. Third-Party Services

The App operates entirely within the Canva platform. Please refer to Canva's Privacy Policy for information about how Canva handles your data.

5. Logo Uploads

If you choose to upload a logo image, this image is processed locally in your browser and inserted directly into your Canva design. It is never uploaded to any external server.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact
